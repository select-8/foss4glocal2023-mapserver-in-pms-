And the Management System?

This began life as a desktop GIS in MapInfo in the 1990s.
Compass first got involved in the project in 2010, creating a web frontend.
At this point it was still installed in each LA on their own web servers and database servers.
While this began to bring some structure to managing pavements in Ireland, over time, the need for a centralised data management system became apparent. To this end, in 2016, Compass, working with the RMO, began development of a web-based system with a centralised data repository.
